Pricing Studio — Support

Pricing Studio is the companion app for the DPYC ecosystem — set and model dynamic pricing for the MCP services you operate, manage your Nostr identity and credentials, and top up satoshi balances over Bitcoin Lightning.

Get help

Email lonniev@gmail.com and we'll get back to you. Please include your app version (Settings → About) and a short description of what you were doing.

Common questions

  • How do I sign in? There is no traditional login — your identity is a Nostr keypair you create in the app. Your secret key stays in your device Keychain.
  • How do payments work? You top up a satoshi balance by paying a Bitcoin Lightning invoice with your own Lightning wallet. The app never holds your funds.
  • Is my data private? Yes — see our Privacy Policy. Credentials are encrypted on-device and shared only over end-to-end encrypted channels.
